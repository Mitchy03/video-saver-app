# Privacy Policy

This application collects minimal device information solely for delivering rewarded advertisements and processing subscriptions via RevenueCat. No downloaded media is transmitted to our servers. Ad and purchase providers may collect diagnostics in accordance with their respective policies.

- Analytics: Basic anonymized usage events (screen views, purchase state) for stability.
- Storage: Downloaded clips remain on the device only.
- Opt-out: Remove the app or disable analytics via OS settings.

For questions, contact support@example.com.
